about me

Hi & welcome to my website! I am so happy to have you here ❤

My name is Sarah and I am 21 years old from Washington State. I am a NASM (National Academy of Sports Medicine) Certified Personal Trainer & Certified Nutrition Coach, and will also be graduating in May 2022 with my Bachelors in Human Development Education. If you know me, you know how passionate I am about taking care of my body & educating others to do the same.

I loved being active from a young age when I started doing gymnastics at 7, which I continued all the way through high school, and picked up pole vault during my high school years as well. After going to college and no longer having any kind of structured physical activity, I found myself feeling very unhappy and not confident with the way I looked, thinking all of that would be fixed if I just lost weight. So I turned to all the “quick fixes”, you know, super low calorie, almost no carbs, hours of ab workouts and cardio, and spent a year being very restrictive with my eating, only to be left even unhappier than before with a horrible relationship with food and very little changes in my physique.

In the middle of 2020 I decided it was time to commit to living a healthier lifestyle. I started fueling my body with more food (and carbs, yum) than ever and stuck to a consistent workout plan that I actually enjoyed, and fell in love with the process. I’ll never forget what it felt like to finally have my mindset switch from wanting to be smaller & working out as a punishment, to wanting to be stronger & working out as a way to take care of myself. This is what I want to help others achieve, because movement is essential and a way for us to celebrate all that our bodies are capable of.
